# Introduction and goals

## High level requirements

- Allow users to collect content from the web that they find interesting to learn more about.
- Automatically summarize content, extracting key conclusions, and actionable insights.
- Generate a weekly podcast to share the insights collected for the past week.

## Quality goals

- Robust workflow for content summarization and podcast generation
- Accessible solution for collecting interesting content from the web
- Easily modifiable because we expect improvements in the AI capabilities
